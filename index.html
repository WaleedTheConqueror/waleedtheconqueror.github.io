About

Hi there! My name is Waleed Raza, and I'm a passionate and dedicated student pursuing a degree in Computer Science. I have a strong interest in software engineering and aspire to build a successful career in this field. In all aspects of my life, I continually aspire to improve my skills, learn as much as I can, and be the best version of myself I can be. My interest in computers started from a young age. In middle school I was introduced to programming, and I enjoyed it from the start. I started with games made in scratch, to webpage development and then to basic python. As my skills and interest grew over the years, I began to build computers, program circuits, and write useful programs to help automize some daily tasks. When it came time to apply for university, I heavily contemplated choosing electrical engineering, computer science and computer engineering as my major. Ultimately, I chose computer science, the reasoning behind this stems from my goals to understand more complex coding techniques and practices, to work with low-level languages like C and to overall become a better programmer. Additionally, I am fascinated by machine learning and artificial intelligence, I believe that this world can truly benefit with the integration of AI into our society, and for me personally pursuing software as a CS major is the best way, I can immerse myself in my greatest interests in this field. While I still maintain a strong interest for the hardware side of computers, as of now my studies are mostly focused on software and data sciences; although I hope to one day educate myself further on electrical/computer engineering. Aside from computers, some things I am passionate about/interested in are Weightlifting and fitness, Animals, Cars, Anime, video games, and hanging out with my close friends/family. While I have many career aspirations and professional goals, I try to recognize the importance of a well-rounded life. I seek self-improvement every day and aim to work hard to enjoy and learn from everything I encounter in this journey of life!
